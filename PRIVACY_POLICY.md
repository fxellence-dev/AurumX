# Privacy Policy for AurumX

**Last Updated**: November 22, 2025

## Overview
AurumX ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Authentication Data
- **Email address**: Collected via Google Sign-In
- **Google User ID**: Used to link your alerts to your account
- **Purpose**: Required for sending price alert notifications

### Alert Data
- **Price alerts you create**: Alert name, currency, price threshold, condition
- **Alert status**: Active/inactive state
- **Purpose**: To monitor prices and send you notifications

### Device Data
- **Expo Push Token**: A unique identifier for sending push notifications to your device
- **Device platform**: iOS
- **Purpose**: To deliver push notifications

## Data We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ No personal information beyond email
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No contact lists
- ❌ No photos or media
- ❌ No payment information (app is free)

## How We Use Your Data
1. **Send Notifications**: We use your email and push token to send price alerts
2. **Authenticate Your Account**: We use Google Sign-In to verify your identity
3. **Store Your Alerts**: We save your custom alerts in a secure database

## Data Storage & Security
- All data stored in Supabase (enterprise-grade security)
- Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- No data sharing with third parties
- No data monetization

## Third-Party Services

### Google Sign-In
- Used for authentication only
- We receive: email address and user ID
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Supabase (Database)
- Hosts your alerts and authentication data
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance
- Supabase Privacy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Expo Push Notifications
- Delivers push notifications to your device
- Expo Privacy: https://expo.dev/privacy

### AWS SES (Email Service)
- Sends email notifications
- AWS Privacy: https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/

### Public Gold Price APIs
- We fetch gold prices from public, free resources
- No personal data shared with these services

## Your Rights

### Access Your Data
You can view all your alerts in the app.

### Delete Your Data
1. Sign in to the app
2. Go to Profile → Settings
3. Tap "Delete Account"
4. All your data will be permanently deleted

### Export Your Data
Contact us at support@aurumx.fxellence.com to request a data export.

## Data Retention
- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Inactive accounts**: Data retained for 2 years, then deleted

## Children's Privacy
AurumX is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
