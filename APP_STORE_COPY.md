# Privacy Policy for AurumX

**Last Updated**: November 22, 2025

- **Price alerts you create**: Alert name, currency, price threshold, condition
- **Alert status**: Active/inactive state
- **Purpose**: To monitor prices and send you notifications

- **Expo Push Token**: A unique identifier for sending push notifications to your device
- **Device platform**: iOS
- **Purpose**: To deliver push notifications

- ‚ùå No personal information beyond email
- ‚ùå No location data

2. **Authenticate Your Account**: We use Google Sign-In to verify your identity
3. **Store Your Alerts**: We save your custom alerts in a secure database

## Data Storage & Security
- All data stored in Supabase (enterprise-grade security)

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- No data sharing with third parties
