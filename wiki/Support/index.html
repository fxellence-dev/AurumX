‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a price alert?
Navigate to the Alerts tab, tap the "+" button, select your currency (GBP, USD, or INR), choose the condition (Above or Below), enter your target price, and tap "Create Alert". You'll receive both push and email notifications when the price reaches your threshold.
Why am I not receiving notifications?
Make sure you've:
  • Enabled push notifications in your iOS Settings ‚Üí AurumX ‚Üí Notifications
  • Verified your email address
  • Turned on the alert toggle in the Alerts tab
  • Checked your email spam/junk folder
Note: Alerts have a 15-minute cooldown to prevent spam.
How often are gold prices updated?
Gold prices are fetched in real-time from reliable public sources. The app checks for price updates regularly and will trigger your alerts as soon as the conditions are met.
Is AurumX really free?
Yes! AurumX is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. There are no ads either. We're committed to keeping it free forever.
What data does AurumX collect?
We collect only what's essential:
  • Your email address (from Google Sign-In for authentication)
  • Your price alerts (stored securely in our database)
  • Your push notification token (to send alerts)
We do NOT collect location data, browsing history, analytics, or any tracking data. Your privacy is our priority.
Can I use AurumX on multiple devices?
Yes! Sign in with the same Google account on multiple iOS devices. Your alerts will sync across all devices, and you'll receive notifications on each device where you're signed in.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including alerts and push tokens. You can also email us at contact@aurumx.fxellence.com to request account deletion.
Where does the gold price data come from?
All gold price data is sourced from reliable, publicly available APIs. We don't provide financial advice - AurumX is purely an informational tool for tracking gold market prices.

üîß Troubleshooting

App won't load or crashes

Try these steps:

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Check your internet connection
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us

Sign-in issues

If you're having trouble signing in with Google:

  • Make sure you're connected to the internet
  • Try signing out of Google in your device settings and sign in again
  • Clear Safari cache (Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí Clear History and Website Data)
  • Check if Google services are working (try signing in elsewhere)

Prices not updating

If gold prices seem outdated:

  • Pull down on the screen to refresh manually
  • Check your internet connection
  • Close and reopen the app
